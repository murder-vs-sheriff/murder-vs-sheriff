## 🐛 Common Issues and Fixes

### ❌ GUI doesn't appear after execution
**Fix:** The panel may be rendering off-screen or behind the
Roblox UI layer. Rejoin the server and execute immediately after
loading in. Minimum recommended resolution is 1280×720 on PC.

### ❌ Aimbot not locking onto targets
**Fix:** Open the aim settings panel and increase the FOV radius.
If targets are still missed, switch the target bone from `Head`
to `HumanoidRootPart` for more reliable lock-on during movement.

### ❌ Kill aura eliminates own team or self
**Fix:** Enable the team-filter toggle in the kill aura settings.
This prevents the aura from targeting players on your assigned
faction during team-based round variants.

### ❌ Auto kill all only targets some players
**Fix:** Players who joined the server after script execution may
not be registered in the initial target list. Switch to the
**auto kill loop** mode instead — it continuously updates and
targets all active players in real time.

### ❌ Fly hacks cause rubber-banding or disconnection
**Fix:** Reduce your fly speed in the settings panel. Extremely
high speed values can trigger the game's movement sanity checks.
A speed of 50–80 units is the stable range for most servers.

### ❌ Script crashes on Android executor
**Fix:** Update your executor to the latest version. Arceus X
and Delta are the most stable options for the mobile build.
Restart both Roblox and the executor before re-attempting.

### ❌ "HttpGet failed" error on load
**Fix:** Verify your internet connection and check that HTTP
request permissions are enabled in your executor's settings.
Some executors require this to be manually toggled on.
